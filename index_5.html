About the Creator

The Story Behind OMBCreates

OMBCreates is a UK-based YouTube creator who launched his channel on 5 March 2020 and quickly built one of the most engaged communities in the Roblox and ERLC space. With over 430,000 subscribers and more than 211 million views, OMB has become a trusted voice for gamers who love Emergency Response: Liberty County.

Beyond raw numbers, OMB is about community first — fostering an active Discord server, contributing to the ERLC Marketplace, and producing content that's genuinely useful and entertaining for fans of all ages.

From humble beginnings to a channel that reaches millions every month, OMBCreates continues to grow, evolve, and inspire the next generation of Roblox content creators.
